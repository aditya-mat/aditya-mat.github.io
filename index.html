About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Chemistry at IIT Delhi, working with Prof. Dibyajyoti Ghosh in computational materials chemistry.

My research focuses on the electronic structure and excited-state phenomena of two-dimensional halide perovskites using first-principles calculations. During my master's thesis I studied hydrogen-bond driven photoluminescence quantum yield (PLQY) enhancement in deep-blue copper-iodide hybrids.

I am broadly interested in computational materials science, data-driven discovery of functional materials, scientific machine learning for materials design, and energy-storage devices.
